The Well

The Story

You were walking to the well outside your house to get the water for the day. You were in a rush and didn't pay attention to the rocks near the well. You trip, and find yourself falling down into the well. You get up, brush yourself off and notice something strange, the well has a tunnel. What will you do?

Choice 1:


Try and climb back out of the well.

Choice 2:


Follow the tunnel.
